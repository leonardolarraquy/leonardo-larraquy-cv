Professional Summary

Leonardo is a seasoned IT professional with over 20 years of experience in software development, team leadership, and business operations. Starting as a Java developer, he has evolved into a senior IT director, successfully leading teams of 30+ professionals across multiple countries.

His career took an international turn when he was expatriated to Colombia as General Manager for Wavy, where he led the entire operation for 3 years before becoming LATAM Business Director. This experience provided him with deep insights into cross-cultural team management and Latin American business dynamics.

Currently serving as IT Director at T-MOBS, Leonardo leads a team of 10 Python developers and 8 operations specialists, driving digital transformation initiatives and ensuring robust infrastructure support.
